About Us

Break IT is a company with some brilliant software engineers having years of experience. We started our journey from December, 2019. Our two founders of the company, are graduated in CSE with an excellent idea of low cost IT solutions for companies with lower revenue but have strong dedication with their passion. We have a team with different expertise in a short period of time with modern technologies.

Objectives

As we are an IT solution company, our main target is to provide top notch solutions for making the beautiful mind into reality. In the recent pandemic condition, people are getting more into online business. A lot of them are starting with a very short amount of capital. Our target is to make their little business touch the sky with great hope and prosperity. As, We have mentioned earlier that we sepcially want to work with the companies with little revenue, our main objective is to provide them IT solutions for a very short charging. We do not think about huge profit rather we take it as a social responsibility. Our motto is to work and going forward together.
